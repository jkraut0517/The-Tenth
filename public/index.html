About The Tenth

The Tenth Massage was founded by Gigi Cunningham.

Gigi Cunningham is a Licensed Massage Therapist (LMT), Registered Yoga Instructor (RYT), and an attuned Reiki master. She specializes in myofascial, neuromuscular, lymphatic facilitation, Swedish, and sports massage.

By focusing on the gentle and slow release of myofascial, visceral, unwinding, craniosacral and energetic techniques, Gigi's multifaceted approach allows her to design unique sessions for each client. Whether treating an injury or easing the stress of everyday life, Gigi understands her clients' needs and can create an appropriate plan for each individual's situation.
