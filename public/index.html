About The Tenth

The Tenth Massage was founded by Gigi Cunningham.

Gigi specializes in myofascial, neuromuscular, lymphatic facilitation, Swedish, and sports massage. In addition to being a licensed massage therapist and graduate of Discovery Point School of Massage, her touch is informed by her other accomplishments in the holistic health field, which include a yoga certification from Shiva Yoga Peeth in Rishikesh and a Reiki Master attunement.

Gigi is currently focusing on the gentle and slow release of myofascial, visceral, unwinding, craniosacral and energetic techniques. This multifaceted approach allows her to design unique sessions for each client. Whether treating an injury or easing the stress of everyday life through a relaxing massage, Gigi understands her clients needs and can create an appropriate plan for each individual's unique situation.
